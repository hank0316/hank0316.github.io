Our new preprint [Transferring Textual Preferences to Vision-Language Understanding through Model Merging](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.13487) is out! We show that text scalar RMs can be merged into Vision LLMs to build VL-RMs. (Update 05/2025: The paper is accepted to ACL 2025 Main.)
